## Troubleshooting

**"Collection ready!" already showing?**
- You're all set! No need to do anything else.

**Setup button not appearing?**
- Make sure your wallet is connected
- Try refreshing the page

**Transaction failed?**
- Ensure you have enough FLOW for gas fees (usually very small, < 0.001 FLOW)
- Check that your wallet is connected to **mainnet** (not testnet)
