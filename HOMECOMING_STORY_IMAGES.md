# Homecoming Story Images

The Homecoming story chapter requires the following images in the `/public/images/cutscenes/` directory:

## Required Images:

### `homecoming-main.png`
- **Purpose**: Thumbnail image for the Homecoming chapter in Story Mode
- **Content**: Should show a gymnasium or dance floor setup for homecoming
- **Suggested**: Streamers, disco balls, decorated gym interior

### `homecoming-1.png`
- **Scene**: Preparation
- **Story Text**: "By Saturday morning, the gym was ready for a night to remember. Streamers hung from the rafters, disco balls cast dancing lights across the polished floor, and everything was perfect for homecoming."
- **Content**: Decorated gymnasium ready for dance

### `homecoming-2.png`
- **Scene**: The Announcement  
- **Story Text**: "But by midday, the announcement came: the dance was off. No one gave a reason, just murmurs about a situation still unfolding. Nobody knew for sure what was happening — only that it wasn't good."
- **Content**: School hallway or office setting with announcement board or officials

### `homecoming-3.png`
- **Scene**: The Mystery
- **Story Text**: "The lights stayed on, the decorations hung in silence, and the dance floor never saw a single step. The mystery of what happened that day still echoes through these empty halls."
- **Content**: Empty, decorated gymnasium with lights on but no people
