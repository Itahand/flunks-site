## üöÄ How to Use

### Step 1: Access My Locker
1. Open the application in your browser
2. Click on the **My Locker** icon on the desktop
3. Connect your wallet or use dev mode (if in development)

### Step 2: Upload Your Background
1. In the locker interior, you'll see two upload sections:
   - **üì∏ PHOTO** - For your profile photo (left side)

### Step 3: Add Text Overlay
Once your background is uploaded, the text overlay controls will appear:

1. **Text Input**: Enter the text you want to overlay on your background
2. **Color Picker**: Choose the color for your text
3. **Size Slider**: Adjust text size (12px - 48px)
4. **Position Sliders**: 
   - **H (Horizontal)**: Move text left/right (10% - 90%)
   - **V (Vertical)**: Move text up/down (10% - 90%)

### Step 4: Preview Your Creation
- A large preview window shows your background with the text overlay
- Changes are reflected in real-time as you adjust settings
- The preview shows exactly how your final image will look

1. Once you're happy with your design, click the **üíæ DOWNLOAD** button

## üé® Tips for Best Results

### Image Selection
- **Square images** (1:1 ratio) work best for balanced compositions
- **High resolution** images (1000x1000px or larger) provide better quality
- **Good contrast** between background and text colors improves readability

### Text Overlay Design
- **Bold colors** like white, black, or bright colors stand out well
- **Simple, short text** is more readable and impactful
- **Positioning**: Try placing text in areas with less visual complexity

### File Formats
- **PNG**: Best for images with transparency or sharp graphics
- **JPG**: Good for photographs and gradients
- **GIF**: Supports animation (static frame will be used)
- **WebP**: Modern format with good compression

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Image Won't Upload
- Check file size is under 10MB
- Ensure the file is a valid image format
- Try refreshing the page and uploading again

### Text Not Showing
- Make sure you've entered text in the input field
- Check if text color is too similar to background
- Adjust text position if it's outside the visible area

### Download Not Working
- Ensure your browser allows downloads
- Try a different browser if issues persist
- Check if you have sufficient storage space

### Performance Issues
- Large images may take time to process
- Consider resizing very large images before upload
- Close other browser tabs to free up memory
